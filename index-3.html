Program Structure

The Executive MBA program is divided into six semesters. Over the first four semesters, the program consists of thirteen theory papers, seven sessional papers, and four laboratory classes, two in business communication, one in leadership development, and one in software lab.

The Choices of 5 Specific Specializations in MBA For Working Executives include:

  • Applied Finance
  • Business Analytics
  • Leadership & Strategy
  • Marketing Management
  • Operations & Supply Chains Management



Eligibility :-

The Applier Requires To Have A Bachelor’s Degree (10+2+3) In Any Discipline From Recognized Universities With A Minimum Of 55% And 3+ Years Of Work Experience.

OR

The Applier Requires To Have Bachelor’s Degree (10+2+3) In Any Discipline From Recognized Universities With A Minimum Of 55% And Post-graduation/professional Degree From Recognized Universities With A Minimum Of 55% And 2+ Years Of Work Experience.
