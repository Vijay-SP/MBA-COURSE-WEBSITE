Eligibility :-

The Applier Requires To Have A Bachelor’s Degree (10+2+3) In Any Discipline From Recognized Universities With A Minimum Of 55% And 3+ Years Of Work Experience.

OR

The Applier Requires To Have Bachelor’s Degree (10+2+3) In Any Discipline From Recognized Universities With A Minimum Of 55% And Post-graduation/professional Degree From Recognized Universities With A Minimum Of 55% And 2+ Years Of Work Experience.

Learning instructions

Listen to recordings of eminent faculty lectures

Content that facilitates interactive learning

In order to ensure that learners retain maximum learning throughout their course, every learning content is designed in such a way that learners do not lose interest. Throughout the virtual lectures, interactive learning content keeps learners engaged and helps them gain maximum insight.

Webinars geared toward industry

The interaction with industry experts is also critical to understanding upcoming trends and demands of the industry, in addition to recorded lectures and course materials. Therefore, we regularly host webinars with top industry leaders to deepen our knowledge.

Facilities for submitting assignments and projects

Students' progress and understanding of the course are constantly checked through course projects. Students can submit projects and assignments online and receive feedback on the progress towards the completion of their projects.

On-demand video lectures

Each of us does not understand everything at once on our own. We all need a guide to help us sail through the process. In an online class, since you cannot be present physically and every day, understanding a topic might seem difficult. At this point, you can request an online lecture on any module/topic you prefer for an in-depth understanding and easy learning.

An assessment system that continuously evaluates performance

Learning should include solving assignments and managing projects in order to ensure complete understanding. You can practice what you've learned and enhance problem-solving skills with live applications. Therefore, each course includes regular tasks, assignments, and project work
